## MACHINERY: WORKS
- Triclinic (hexagonal) cell accepted after fixing AA_PLANAR X_XTNT to the lattice dim
  (12.836, not the 19.254 Cartesian bounding box -> earlier "extents outside range" abort).
- Both Dirichlet plates parsed; V_D -3/+3 V -> -/+0.1102 Eh/e (correct).
- Implicit Poisson solver converges EVERY SCF cycle (~60-127 iters cold, warm-starts after).
- Runs under RUN_TYPE MD (NVT).

## ELECTRONIC SCF: UNRELIABLE (the blocker)
Per-MD-step SCF cycle counts and wall times:
  step 0 (MD_INI): converged 30 cycles  (cold ATOMIC guess, ~5 min)
  step 1:          converged 15 cycles  (404 s wall)
  step 2:          converged 33 cycles  (102 s wall)
  step 3:          FAILED at 150 cycles (MAX_SCF) -> CP2K ABORTED the run
- The +-3 V field + metallic slab makes Broyden mixing slosh; convergence plateaus near
  few x 1e-4 and intermittently cannot reach EPS_SCF 1e-5 within MAX_SCF -> abort.

## COST
- 100-400 s/step on 96 ranks for the *converging* steps = ~5-18x plain AIMD (~22 s/step/64r).
- A 10 ps biased trajectory would be ~17-46 days AND would abort on the first bad SCF step.
  => NOT production-ready as-is.

## FIXES TO TRY BEFORE PRODUCTION (ranked)
1. Soften the field: +-1 V (0.04 V/A) likely converges far more stably; scan up from there.
2. SCF hardening: more ADDED_MOS, &OUTER_SCF wrapper, PULAY or tuned-Broyden mixing,
   MAX_SCF 300, and/or a slightly looser EPS_SCF (1e-5 -> 5e-5) for biased runs only.
3. IGNORE_CONVERGENCE_FAILURE to survive occasional bad steps (use with care; check forces).
4. Consider single-point bias scans (RUN_TYPE ENERGY) for the field/charge response first,
   reserving MD for one well-converged bias.

## BOTTOM LINE
The constant-V Dirichlet counter-electrode approach is the right (and only) path available in
CP2K 2025.1 (no ESM, no grand-canonical constant-mu; see literature memo) and the MACHINERY is
proven to run under MD. But the electronic SCF needs hardening + a gentler field before a
production biased trajectory is affordable. Honest framing for any report: this is an idealized
constant-VOLTAGE counter-electrode, not grand-canonical constant-mu on the metal.
