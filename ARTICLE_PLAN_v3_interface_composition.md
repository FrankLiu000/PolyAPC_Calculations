## PART A ‚ÄĒ RE-ANALYSIS OF ALL WET-LAB RESULTS (evidence base)
APC ‚Č° AlCl‚āÉ + PhMgCl in THF ‚Üí cation **[Mg‚āā(őľ-Cl)‚āÉ(THF)‚āÜ]‚Āļ**, anion family **[Al‚āďPhŠĶßCl_z]‚ĀĽ**. poly-APC = APC polymerised in-situ with **POSS** (silsesquioxane network). Two electrodes compared throughout: **bare** (Mg cycled in liquid APC) vs **poly** (Mg cycled in polymerised APC).

### A1. ToF-SIMS (negative ions, Cs‚Āļ sputter, 50 ¬Ķm FOV) ‚ÄĒ **the new centrepiece**
Element-filtered, separator-corrected (B = GF/D-separator artifact, excluded). poly/bare intensity ratios (`processed/data/diagnostic_ions_corrected.csv`):
| family | species (poly/bare ratio) | reading |
|---|---|---|
| **Si (POSS)** ‚ÜĎ | Si‚ĀĽ **√ó20**, SiO‚āā‚ĀĽ **√ó30**, SiO‚āÉ‚ĀĽ **√ó34** | poly SEI strongly **Si-enriched** (POSS network) |
| **Al** ‚Üď‚Üď | AlO‚ĀĽ **√ó0.13**, AlO‚āā‚ĀĽ √ó0.26, Al‚ĀĽ √ó0.08, **Al‚āā‚ĀĽ √ó0.02**, Al‚āāO‚āÉ‚ĀĽ √ó0.21, MgAlO‚ĀĽ √ó0.18, C‚āāAl‚ĀĽ √ó0.35 | poly SEI **Al-depleted** across every Al ion |
| Mg/Cl/O/organics | MgO‚ĀĽ √ó1.6, MgCl‚ĀĽ √ó2.0, Cl‚ĀĽ √ó1.1, O‚ĀĽ √ó1.06, C‚āā‚ĀĽ √ó2.6 | comparable inorganic + slightly more organic in poly |
**Depth (x50, signal halves):** bare AlO‚ĀĽ **350 nm**, AlO‚āā‚ĀĽ 224 nm, total 275 nm; poly AlO‚ĀĽ **92 nm**, AlO‚āā‚ĀĽ 60 nm, SiO‚āā‚ĀĽ 57, SiO‚āÉ‚ĀĽ 48, total 206 nm. ‚Üí **poly confines SEI species to a sharp ~50‚Äď90 nm surface layer; bare's Al penetrates ~3‚Äď4√ó deeper.**
**Net:** poly = **Si-rich, Al-poor, surface-confined** SEI; bare = **Si-poor, Al-rich, deep** SEI. *(Exactly the user's directed framing ‚ÄĒ robustly supported.)*

### A3. XRD of deposited Mg (Mg@PP separator, Cu KőĪ)
Both = metallic hcp-Mg; **no MgO(200) 42.9¬į** ‚Üí oxide-free deposits. **bare:** Mg(101) tallest, texture ‚Čą random (A002/A101 = 0.39), full higher-angle family ‚Üí **coarse, randomly-oriented, protruding** Mg. **poly:** **texture-enhanced** (A002/A101 = 0.83 ‚Čą 2‚Äď3√ó random), larger apparent crystallite (~21 vs ~11 nm) ‚Üí **thin, conformal, basal/prismatic-oriented** Mg ("the good sign"). **No crystalline Al / Mg‚ÄďAl phase** ‚Üí co-deposited Al in bare is **amorphous / SEI-bound** (seen by surface XPS/ToF-SIMS, not bulk XRD). *(Caveat: normalise by areal capacity before claiming "less Mg".)*

### A4. Multi-rate CV + GITT (Mg‚ÄĖMo‚āÜS‚āą) ‚ÄĒ reversibility, and **D is NOT the discriminator**
- **Coulombic efficiency:** poly ‚Čą **100%** (GITT 106%), bare = **27%** (167 mAh g‚ĀĽ¬Ļ charge for 46 discharge). bare OCV **turns over ~1.29 V then sags**; bare **self-discharges ‚ąí320 mV (up to ‚ąí540) per 1 h rest**; poly relaxes ‚ąí100 mV to a stable OCV.
- **CV:** poly |i_pa/i_pc| ‚Čą **1.0** vs bare **1.2‚Äď1.34**; poly OCP stable ~1.36 V vs bare collapsing **1.12 ‚Üí 0.50 V**.
- **Mg¬≤‚Āļ chemical diffusion (GITT):** poly **9√ó10‚ĀĽ¬Ļ‚ĀĶ**, bare **8√ó10‚ĀĽ¬Ļ‚ĀĶ cm¬≤ s‚ĀĽ¬Ļ** ‚ÄĒ **near-identical** ‚Üí **bulk transport is NOT the difference**, independently confirming MD (poly 4.4√ó *slower*, t‚āä = 0.50). Discriminator = **reversibility + interfacial stability**.

### A5. Earlier techniques (all consistent)
SEM: bare rough/dendritic, poly smooth. Aurbach CE: poly 95.2%, bare ‚Č•100% soft-short. CCD: poly stable to 3.0 mA cm‚ĀĽ¬≤, bare 3.1 V breakdown. Cycling: poly 842 cyc @0.5C / 3344 @1C, bare fades. In-situ DRT: poly higher-but-**stable** interfacial impedance. Raman: Mg¬≤‚Āļ first-shell THF/Cl coordination **preserved**, gel adds polyether/POSS modes (915 retained, 1002‚Üí1034 shift). MD: D ‚Üď4.4√ó, t‚āä 0.50, cation segregated ~7 √Ö. Prior DFT: anion oxidation IP 6.18 eV; POSS most oxidation-resistant; desolvation ladder; ether-O migration relay.

### A6. The single coherent picture
**bare-APC:** Al-anion is **reduced at the Mg anode ‚Üí Al co-deposits** (metallic/alloyed Al‚Āį; Al-rich SEI ~350 nm deep) ‚Üí coarse, randomly-oriented Mg ‚Üí dendrites, soft-shorts, **overcharge & self-discharge (CE 27%)**.
**poly-APC:** the cured POSS network builds a **thin, Si-rich, Al-poor, surface-confined SEI** that **suppresses Al-anion reduction / Al co-deposition** (only oxidised Al¬≥‚Āļ residue) ‚Üí thin, conformal, oriented Mg ‚Üí smooth, **reversible (CE ~100%)**, stable, high CCD, long cycling.
**Transport is identical** (GITT & MD); the win is **interfacial composition (Si-rich, Al-poor).**

## PART B ‚ÄĒ THE NEW ARTICLE NARRATIVE (ŤĄČÁĽú)

**Working title:** *"Interphase-by-design in magnesium batteries: an in-situ POSS network builds a silicon-rich, aluminium-poor interphase that suppresses Al co-deposition."*

**One-sentence thesis:** Polymerising APC in-situ with POSS does not change how fast Mg¬≤‚Āļ moves; it changes **what the Mg-anode interphase is made of** ‚ÄĒ converting an Al-rich, dendrite-prone, parasitic SEI into a thin, Si-rich, Al-poor passivating layer ‚ÄĒ and that compositional switch is what makes Mg plating reversible.

**Graphical abstract:** two anodes. bare: Al-anion + e‚ĀĽ ‚Üí Al‚Āį co-deposited (Al-rich deep SEI), rough Mg, dendrite/short. poly: POSS surface layer; Si-rich thin SEI; Al excluded (Al¬≥‚Āļ only); smooth oriented Mg. Centre: "interphase re-programming ‚ÄĒ Si in, Al out."

## PART E ‚ÄĒ CAVEATS & OPEN QUESTIONS
1. **"Less Al" is depth/chemical-state, not surface-at%.** XPS surface Al at% is similar (poly 4.11 vs bare 3.83); the claim "poly ÁēĆťĚĘ Al śõīŚįĎ" is supported by **(i)** ToF-SIMS depth-integrated Al ions √ó0.02‚Äď0.5 + shallow confinement and **(ii)** the Al 2p state (oxidised residue vs metallic co-deposit). 2. **XRD can't see amorphous/dilute Al** ‚ÄĒ absence of a crystalline Mg‚ÄďAl phase does NOT contradict Al co-deposition (surface/amorphous). Use XRD only for Mg morphology/texture.
3. **Boron is a GF/D-separator artifact ‚ÄĒ exclude.** Silicon has a separator baseline too; only the **poly excess** (√ó20‚Äď34) is POSS.
4. **XRD intensity ‚Č† amount** ‚ÄĒ confirm morphology at matched areal capacity.
5. **Honest spine:** transport is genuinely *not* the discriminator (triply shown: MD, GITT, CV). The paper's value is locating & explaining the **interfacial-compositional (Si-rich, Al-poor)** origin.
