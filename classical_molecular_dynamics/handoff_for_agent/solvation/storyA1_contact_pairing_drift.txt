Interpretation
--------------
* Both systems START ~57-64% paired (NPT-equilibrated structures are under-associated)
  and PAIR UP during production. The 1 ns pre-equilibration does NOT equilibrate ion
  association: bare plateaus ~80 ns (~95%), swollen ~40 ns (~82-83%).
* This reconciles the previously-inconsistent contact numbers, all at the SAME cutoff:
    - campaign bare_solv_summary.txt  49.7%  ~ first ~5 ns
    - MANIFEST headline                83.3%  ~ mid-window
    - naive last-50 ns read            94%    ~ late/bare-converged
  Nobody used a wrong cutoff; they sampled different points on an unconverged transient.
* bare r1 (original) and bare r4 (new seed) drift identically -> systematic/reproducible.

Converged free-carrier fraction f (70-100 ns window, mean +/- SEM over 5 reps):
    bare-APC      f = 0.046 +/- 0.006   (95.4% paired)
    8-POSS swollen f = 0.155 +/- 0.005   (84.5% paired)
    -> swollen de-pairs ~3.4x vs bare.

Consequence for protocol: use >=50 ns pre-equilibration in all subsequent stories, and read
f (and ideally D) from the converged window only. See reports/REPORT_storyA1.md.
